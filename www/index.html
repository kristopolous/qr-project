Project Poetry LA

Bringing public art into public space

Classic poetry and art as stickers
to boost someone's day

A non-commercial project to help build community.
Originally out of Los Angeles but now in 5 confirmed cities.

QR Codes

Large QR codes that when scanned, reveal classic works of poetry. Re-imagine what your smart-phone experience could be like and the kind of content you expose yourself to.

Traditional Text

Poetry fit into a 4" x 3.33" form-factor in languages as diverse as Los Angeles. Take some time out of your day to smell the flowers and read the sonnet.

Classic Paintings

Culled from the National Gallery of Art, these small reproductions help remind how close at hand great works of art are if we only choose to enjoy them. On the internet, or at our many local museums.

Free and Reproducible

Follow the instructions to print out your own series or send us your mailing address for a set of free complementary stickers. We also encourage people to place sticker sheets in Little Free Libraries.
