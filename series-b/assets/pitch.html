You hold in your hands

A way to share art and poetry in your local community.

These six stickers are made to be placed out in the open for all to enjoy. Think of a quiet place that's special to you or of one that's people are too busy walking by to pay attention to.

Bring focus to that space and bring focus to this art. Encourage the development of non-commercial community space through gentle acts.

Get permission where needed and always be respectful.
