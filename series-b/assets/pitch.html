You Hold in Your Hands

A way to share art and poetry in our community.

These six stickers are made to be placed out in the open for all to see. Think of quiet places special to you or ones people pretend to be too busy for.

Bring focus to that space. Bring focus to this art. Encourage the development of non-commercial community through gentle acts.

Get permission where needed and always be respectful.

Thank you.
